Swimwear Size Guide

How to Measure for Swimwear

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape measure level
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, typically the narrowest part of your torso
  • Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, typically 8-9 inches below your waist
  • Underbust: Measure directly under your bust, around your ribcage

Swimwear Fit Notes:

Our swimwear is designed with a luxurious embossed texture and offers medium support. The pieces are true to size with slight stretch. For the most comfortable fit, we recommend choosing your regular size. If you prefer a looser fit or are between sizes, consider sizing up.

Accessories Size Guide

Bucket Hats

How to Measure Your Head

  • Use a flexible measuring tape or string
  • Measure around your head, about 1 inch above your eyebrows
  • The tape should sit comfortably, not too tight or loose
  • For the most accurate measurement, have someone help you
Size Head Circumference (inches) Head Circumference (cm) Best For
S 21.5 - 22.5 54.5 - 57 Smaller heads, teens
M 22.5 - 23.5 57 - 59.5 Most adults, average fit
L 23.5 - 24.5 59.5 - 62 Larger heads, looser fit preference

Bucket Hat Fit Notes:

Our bucket hats feature a relaxed, unstructured design that sits comfortably on the head. The lightweight upholstery fabric provides structure while remaining breathable. Most customers find their regular hat size fits perfectly.

General Fit Guide

Finding Your Perfect Fit

  • Know Your Measurements: Always measure yourself before ordering. Body measurements are more accurate than garment sizes from other brands
  • Consider the Fabric: Our embossed swimwear has slight stretch, while our bucket hats are structured but comfortable
  • Read Product Descriptions: Each product page includes specific fit details and fabric information
  • When in Doubt: Contact our customer service team for personalized sizing advice

Care Instructions for Optimal Fit

  • Swimwear: Rinse in cold water after each use, hand wash with mild detergent, lay flat to dry
  • Bucket Hats: Spot clean when needed, reshape while damp, air dry away from direct sunlight
  • Storage: Store swimwear flat, hang bucket hats to maintain shape
