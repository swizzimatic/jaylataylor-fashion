Orders & Checkout

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  • Browse our collections and select your desired items
  • Choose your size and color options
  • Click "Add to Cart" and continue shopping or proceed to checkout
  • Enter your shipping and payment information
  • Review your order and click "Complete Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation email once your purchase is complete.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All payments are processed securely through Stripe with SSL encryption.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After this time, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. To modify or cancel an order, please contact us immediately at orders@jaylataylor.com or call (555) 123-4567.

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs depend on your location and chosen shipping method:

  • Standard Shipping: $25 (5-7 business days)
  • Express Shipping: $45 (2-3 business days)
  • Overnight Shipping: $75 (next business day)

Free shipping is available on orders over $500!

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your package by:

  • Clicking the tracking link in your confirmation email
  • Visiting the carrier's website directly
  • Contacting our customer service team

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping options include:

  • Standard International: $35 (7-14 business days)
  • Express International: $65 (3-7 business days)

Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs duties and taxes.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in original condition. Items must be:

  • Unworn and unwashed
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Swimwear must have hygiene strips intact
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery

For complete details, please visit our Returns Policy page.

How do I start a return or exchange?

To initiate a return or exchange:

  • Contact us at returns@jaylataylor.com or call (555) 123-4567
  • Provide your order number and reason for return
  • We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label
  • Package your items and attach the return label
  • Drop off at any authorized shipping location

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your returned items. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: Typically instant
  • Apple/Google Pay: 3-5 business days

Products & Sizing

How do I know what size to order?

We recommend checking our detailed Size Guide which includes:

  • Measurement charts for all product categories
  • How-to-measure instructions
  • Fit notes for each product type
  • Size conversion charts

If you're still unsure, our customer service team can provide personalized sizing advice.

What materials are your products made from?

Our products are crafted from high-quality materials:

  • Swimwear: 85% Nylon / 15% Spandex with embossed texture
  • Bucket Hats: 100% Polyester lightweight upholstery fabric
  • Linings: 100% Polyester for comfort and durability

All fabric compositions are listed on individual product pages.

How should I care for my Jayla Taylor products?

Care instructions vary by product type:

  • Swimwear: Rinse in cold water after use, hand wash with mild detergent, lay flat to dry
  • Bucket Hats: Spot clean when needed, reshape while damp, air dry away from direct sunlight

Detailed care instructions are included with each product and on product pages.

Account & Website

Do I need to create an account to make a purchase?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account provides benefits like:

  • Faster checkout for future orders
  • Order history and tracking
  • Wishlist functionality
  • Exclusive offers and early sale access
  • Account management and preferences

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  • Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?"
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for reset instructions
  • Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the reset email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact us for assistance.

How do I subscribe to your newsletter?

You can subscribe to our newsletter by:

  • Entering your email in the newsletter signup form on our homepage
  • Checking the newsletter opt-in during checkout
  • Contacting our customer service team

Newsletter subscribers receive exclusive offers, early access to new collections, and fashion updates.
