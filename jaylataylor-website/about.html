About Jayla Taylor

From the moment she picked up a needle at age 10, Jayla Taylor knew she was destined to be a designer. Taught to hand sew by her grandmother, her journey began with creativity rooted in tradition. Recognizing her passion and natural talent, her mother encouraged her to pursue fashion professionally—leading Jayla to earn her degree in Fashion Design.

Jayla later launched her namesake brand, Jayla Taylor, built on the foundation of timeless design with a forward-thinking edge. Her collections span multiple categories—from luxury lingerie and swimwear to statement accessories and emerging menswear pieces—each merging elegance with innovation while maintaining a distinct, architectural aesthetic that appeals to diverse style sensibilities.

Her work has graced runways at New York and Paris Fashion Week, and has been featured in both domestic and international publications. With inspiration drawn from architecture and asymmetrical forms, Jayla continues to push creative boundaries through versatile design that transcends traditional gender boundaries, creating pieces that are both artful and wearable for a sophisticated, diverse clientele.

Design Philosophy

Timeless Elegance

Creating pieces that transcend seasonal trends, focusing on enduring style and sophisticated silhouettes that empower individuals regardless of gender, from intimate luxuries to bold statement pieces for all.

Sustainable Luxury

Committed to ethical production practices and sustainable materials without compromising on quality or luxury.

Artistic Innovation

Pushing boundaries through creative collaborations and innovative techniques while honoring traditional craftsmanship, exploring new territories in fashion from accessories to menswear with the same architectural precision.
