Processing Time

  • Orders placed Monday-Friday before 2 PM EST ship the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends ship the next business day
  • Custom or made-to-order items require 2-3 business days for processing
  • During peak seasons (holidays), processing may take up to 3-5 business days

Shipping Areas

We currently ship to all 50 U.S. states, Puerto Rico, and U.S. territories. International shipping options are available for select countries.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package through our website or directly with the carrier.

Packaging & Sustainability

All orders are carefully packaged in our signature packaging using eco-friendly materials. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact while ensuring your items arrive in perfect condition.

Returns Policy

30-Day Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided items are in original condition.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following criteria:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • All original tags and packaging must be included
  • Swimwear must have hygiene strips intact
  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Swimwear with removed hygiene strips
  • Items purchased with final sale discounts (marked as such)
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Items returned after 30 days

Return Process

1
Initiate Return

Contact us at returns@jaylataylor.com or call (555) 123-4567 to start your return

2
Receive Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours

3
Package & Ship

Pack items securely in original packaging and attach the return label

4
Receive Refund

Refund processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your return

Refund Information

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds take 3-5 business days to appear
  • PayPal refunds are typically instant
  • Original shipping charges are not refundable (unless item was defective)
  • Return shipping is free when using our provided label

Exchanges

Easy Size & Color Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We make exchanges simple and fast.

Exchange Eligibility

Exchanges are available for:

  • Size exchanges (same item, different size)
  • Color exchanges (same item, different color)
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Items received in error

Exchange Process

1
Contact Customer Service

Email exchanges@jaylataylor.com with your order number and exchange request

2
Confirm Availability

We'll check stock availability and confirm your exchange within 4 hours

3
Ship Original Item

Send the original item back using our prepaid return label

4
Receive New Item

Your exchange ships once we receive your original item

Exchange Notes

  • Exchanges must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • If the new item costs more, you'll be charged the difference
  • If the new item costs less, you'll receive a refund for the difference
  • Expedited exchanges available for $25 (new item ships before return received)
  • Out-of-stock items can be backordered or fully refunded

Defective Item Policy

If you receive a defective item, we'll provide a prepaid return label and priority replacement at no cost to you. Contact us immediately for the fastest resolution.
